Unit 8

This is the page showcasing Unit 8 - Mobile App Development.

Unit 8 was an assignment based unit, which was filled with short tasks and three assignments to be completed throughout the lessons we had.

We usually spent 5 hours a week working on Unit 8, with an hour and a half on a Monday and 3 and a half hours on a Friday. In my opinion, this was not much time for the amount of work we actually had involved.

This unit focused on mobile applications, including designs, functionalities, requirements and accessibility options.

All of these small tasks would then lead onto the main assignment, which is building a mobile app from scratch. The app was for 3-7 year olds, teaching them the first 6 letters of the alphabet (A-F).

I was able to obtain a Pass in this Unit following 3 months of work.
