Unit 19

This is the page showcasing Unit 19 - Computing in the Workplace.

Unit 19 is about applying for jobs in the IT sector and is split into two key sections: applying for a job and social media in the workplace. This unit helps you understand the crucial steps involved in securing an IT role and how to manage your professional online presence.

Throughout Unit 19, we had 3 assignments which were all related to learning about, well, computing in the workplace and the world of work in general. We learned about characteristics of employees, how to apply for a job (including a digital cover letter and resume), and how a workplace utilises technology for their work.
